## Selected Design: Response 2 — Bioluminescent Dark Science

**Rationale:** The bioluminescent neural network aesthetic perfectly embodies AxonAI's dual identity — medical precision and AI intelligence. The teal-green accent evokes both clinical trust and technological innovation, while the violet secondary accent reinforces the neural/AI connotation. Glassmorphism cards and animated neural network background create the "flowing" tech feel requested, and the deep navy-black background provides the dark, high-contrast canvas needed for a premium medical AI product.
